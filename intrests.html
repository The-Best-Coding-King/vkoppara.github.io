My Interests
My interests revolve around using the digital world not just to consume, but to create and explore. Technology fascinates me because of its endless possibilities, and I use it to express myself and learn in unique ways. Whether it’s through habit tracking, designing layouts on Canva, or learning new languages on Duolingo, I’m always looking for new tools and platforms that help me grow and discover more. Creativity is at the core of everything I do. I see the digital space as a canvas, where I can design and build projects that reflect who I am and where I want to go. For me, creativity isn’t just about the final product—it's about the process, the learning, and the evolution along the way. Every project is an opportunity to improve, to try new things, and to challenge myself. I also value meaningful online expression. It’s not just about being present on the internet—it’s about shaping my digital identity and making it a true reflection of who I am. Everything I do online connects to a bigger purpose: to be more intentional, to learn consistently, and to grow creatively. My digital world isn’t flashy, but it’s full of structure and thought, always evolving as I explore new possibilities and build something meaningful.
